About Us

Honor is a smartphone brand owned by Shenzhen Zhixin New Information Technology Co., Ltd. It was formerly owned by Huawei Technologies. As part of the former owner Huawei Consumer Business Group's dual-brand strategy, Honor provides smartphone handsets targeting young consumers but has released tablet computers and wearable technology as well. As of 2016, George Zhao serves as global president of Honor. On 17 November 2020, Honor was acquired by Shenzhen Zhixin New Information Technology Co., Ltd.
